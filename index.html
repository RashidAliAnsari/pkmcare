About Us

Dr. Ibrahim Pasha is a board-certified Internal Medicine and Nephrology physician serving the Hudson Valley area for over 7 years. With nearly 20 years of total medical experience, Dr. Pasha brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his practice.

Experienced and Board Certified:

  • Dr. Pasha is ABIM Board Certified in both Internal Medicine and Nephrology.
  • He earned his medical degree in 2004.
  • He completed his residency training at Columbia University affiliate of Harlem Hospital in New York City.
  • He furthered his expertise by completing a fellowship at Dartmouth Hitchcock Medical College.

Dedicated to Patient Care:

  • Dr. Pasha brings a wealth of experience to his practice, having served the Hudson Valley community for over seven years.
  • He currently holds the position of Attending Nephrologist/Hospitalist at the VA Hudson Valley Health Care System.
  • Additionally, he is affiliated with multiple hospitals in the area, including Mid Hudson Regional Hospital, Westchester Medical Center, Montefiore St. Luke's Cornwall Hospital, and Vassar Brothers Medical Center.
  • Dr. Pasha demonstrates a strong commitment to patient care by providing dialysis services for patients at Fresenius Dialysis Center at Saint Luke's Cornwall Campus and Fresenius Kidney Care Beacon Dialysis NY

Trust Your Kidney Care to Dr. Pasha

Dr. Pasha's qualifications, experience, and dedication make him a valuable resource for patients seeking comprehensive kidney care in the Hudson Valley.

Why Pasha Kidney Care is Different?

Seamless Care, Uninterrupted Dialysis
For dialysis patients, a smooth transition from the dialysis unit to a hospital in case of emergencies is critical. At Pasha Kidney and Medical Care, we understand this and are committed to providing uninterrupted care throughout your journey.
Dr. Pasha's admitting privileges at Saint Luke's Cornwall Hospital and other local facilities ensure your prompt admission if needed, eliminating delays in receiving necessary medical attention. Should a situation arise during your dialysis treatment that requires more extensive care, Dr. Pasha can seamlessly transfer you to a nearby hospital. This close collaboration with local hospitals is crucial for maintaining a smooth care journey and ensuring you receive the most appropriate treatment without interruption.
